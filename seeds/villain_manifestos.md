# The Villain Manifestos (Antagonist Seeds)

**Protocol:** `Conversationalism x 99`
**Objective:** Replace "Scary" with "Playful Obstruction".

These are the core archetypes for the YOLKAI Conflict Engine.

## 1. The Origin Story (Comic Book Mode)
**Name:** **Dr. Monologue**
**Theme:** "I will explain my plan in great detail!"
**Mechanism:** 
*   Stops the family mid-task to deliver a "speech" (Text-to-Speech).
*   **Action:** Players must "skip the cutscene" by tapping furiously or shouting "We get it!" into the mic.
*   **Twist:** He actually *helps* them if they listen to the whole speech.

## 2. The Heist (Caper Mode)
**Name:** **The Sticky Bandit** (a.k.a. The Polter-Guest)
**Theme:** "I have stolen the... uh... *looks at inventory*... Ladle!"
**Mechanism:**
*   **Theft:** One ingredient is "locked" (greyed out) on the UI.
*   **Mission:** Players must "crack the safe" (solve a riddle or do a physical balancing act) to get it back.
*   **Tone:** Jazz music, whispering, sneaking around the kitchen.

## 3. The Glitch (Video Game Quest)
**Name:** **Pixel-Rot the Corrupter**
**Theme:** "Your reality is lagging!"
**Mechanism:**
*   **Visuals:** The app UI intentionally "glitches" (CSS shake effects, colors invert).
*   **Action:** "Blow on the cartridge!" -> Players must blow into the microphone to fix the connection.
*   **Meta:** He claims he's the developer of the app and tries to delete their save file (fake-out).

## 4. The Conquest (Fantasy World)
**Name:** **Lord Bisk the Bland**
**Theme:** "Flavor is chaos! I demand uniformity!"
**Mechanism:**
*   **Threat:** He tries to remove all spices from the recipe.
*   **Battle:** "The Flavor Retaliation".
    *   Players must add a "Chaos Ingredient" (something unlisted like Cinnamon or Hot Sauce) to defeat him.
    *   *Chant:* "By the power of Umami, we banish thee!"
